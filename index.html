About The Conference

The 1st International Conference on Communication management, Soft Computing, and Digital Economy(ICCSDE) is focusing on all aspects of Computer Communication, Computer Networks, Soft Computing, and Digital Economy.
The objective of ICCSDE 2021 is to provide a platform forresearchers, engineers, academicians, Economists as well as industrial professionals from all over the world to present their research results and development activities in Advanced Computer technology and Digital Economy.This conference provides opportunities to delegates for exchanging new ideas and experiences face to face, to establish business or research relations, and to find global partners for future collaboration.
